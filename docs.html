PropXcel — QR to WhatsApp Investment Guide Funnel

How Your System Works

Your PropXcel funnel automatically captures leads, delivers your investment guide via WhatsApp, and follows up with a 3-message drip sequence to book strategy review calls. Everything runs on autopilot.

1
Capture
Lead fills the form

A visitor scans your QR code or visits your landing page. They enter their name, email, and phone number. The data is instantly saved to your Google Sheet.

2
Deliver
Guide sent via WhatsApp

Within seconds, they receive your Investment Guide on WhatsApp with a download button. Two minutes later, they get a strategy review invitation.

3
Convert
Automated follow-ups

If they don't book a call, they receive a follow-up after 24 hours and a final message after 72 hours. If they book, follow-ups stop automatically.

Calendly Integration

All WhatsApp buttons link to your Calendly booking page. The system checks Calendly before sending follow-up messages.

📅
propxcel.com.au/book
Redirects to calendly.com/propxcel-info/15min

When someone books a 15-minute Initial Consultation, the system detects it and stops sending follow-up messages to that person.

How the smart check works

Before sending Message 3 (24h) and Message 4 (72h), the system queries the Calendly API using the lead's email address. If a booking is found, the message is skipped. This means:

  • Lead books after Message 1 → Messages 3 and 4 are NOT sent
  • Lead books after Message 3 → Message 4 is NOT sent
  • Lead never books → All 4 messages are sent

How to Make Changes

✎
Change landing page text or design

Go to VentraIP → cPanel → File Manager → public_html/guide/index.html. Click Edit, make changes, and Save. Changes are live immediately.

💬
Change WhatsApp message text

WhatsApp messages use Meta-approved templates. To change the text: create a new template in Meta Business Manager, wait for approval (24-48h), then update the template name in the n8n workflow.

⏰
Change message timing

The delay between messages is set in the n8n workflow Wait nodes. Current settings: 2 minutes (Msg 2), 24 hours (Msg 3), 72 hours (Msg 4). These can be adjusted in n8n without needing Meta approval.

📄
Change the PDF guide

Upload the new PDF to VentraIP → public_html/guide/ with the same filename (PropXcel_Investment_Property_Guide.pdf). If the filename changes, update the WhatsApp template too.

🔗
Change the booking page URL

If you change from Calendly to another booking tool, you'll need new WhatsApp templates with the new button URL, plus update the Calendly check in the n8n workflow.

Troubleshooting

❌
Lead says they didn't receive WhatsApp messages

Check: Is the phone number correct (with country code)? Does the person have WhatsApp on that number? Ask them to search for "PropXcel" or "+61469304387" in their WhatsApp chats. WhatsApp may also limit marketing messages — a fresh number that hasn't received many messages will always work.

❌
Form submits but nothing happens

Check: The n8n workflow must be active. Log into n8n Cloud and verify the workflow is toggled on (green). Also check the webhook URL in the landing page HTML matches the n8n webhook path.

❌
Leads not appearing in Google Sheets

Check: The Google Sheets OAuth token may have expired. Log into n8n Cloud, go to Credentials, and reconnect the Google Sheets account. The sheet tab name must also match exactly (currently "Sheet1").

❌
Follow-ups sent even after booking

Check: The Calendly integration matches leads by email. If the lead used a different email on the form vs. Calendly, the system can't match them. Ensure the booking email matches the form email.
